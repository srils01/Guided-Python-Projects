Welcome to Kalabhinaya Dance Academy

Dance,Art,Passion

Kalabhinaya has been a strong proponent of fostering Indian culture and heritage by teachings of mythology through dance and spreading knowledge by way of performances. As one of the pioneer Bharatnatyam institutions in the tri-state area, we strive to keep our culture alive and thriving by sticking to our niche.Kalabhinaya is beyond language barriers and is focussed on the sprirituality of the art form rather than the medium in which it is rendered.

Classes

Kuchipudi Training

  • Classical track training is approximately 6-8 years and consists of Pushpanjali, Allaripu, Kouthvam, Jathiswaram, Shabdam, Varnam, Padam1, Padam2, Padam3 and Thillana with an option for Arangetram.
  • This class meets once a week, and students are given the opportunity to perform at classical dance programs.
  • The completion of the course is solely dependent on the students ability and determination.

Fees

  • Fees are collected once in a month. There is a one-time registration fee of $75 for new students, non refundable.
  • Fees structure for regular weekly Kuchipudi is $120/month
